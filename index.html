About Me

Hello! I'm Alankrith Shankar, a strategic thinker and product owner with a passion for creative problem solving.
I've spent the last five years delivering digital products that positively impact people's lives. My journey has taken me from the bustling city of Mumbai to the historic city of Sheffield, where I'm currently enhancing my business acumen through an MBA program.

My expertise lies in understanding the nuances of game mechanics and product features, which has made me a go-to expert in my field. I've storyboarded and led the development of six healthcare training products, enhancing their functionality by 75% and consistently delivering major features with minimal bugs.

I've also had the privilege of working with globally renowned corporate and healthcare clients in both B2B and D2C settings. My responsibilities have included everything from brainstorming and building customer relationships to writing user stories, conducting AB testing, and making analytics-based decisions.

By: Admin 456 264

Project Description:

A highly customised product that is built to simulate a tactical and high-pressure scenario that involves saving the life of a K9. The product is targeted at K9 Handlers and Medics. The simulator is built to run on a dedicated LMS, hosted on a web browser. The product is effective in training K9 Medics and Handlers on emergency tactical protocols and veterinary diagnostics by mimicking real-world actions.

There are 2 modules within the product. Each module is targeted at a different user base (handler or medic). Depending on the user profile, the module will provide a tailored experience using narrative and immersive simulation techniques. Knowledge retention is tested using Pre-test and Post-test KPIs.
